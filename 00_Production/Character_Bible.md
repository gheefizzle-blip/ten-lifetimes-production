## Permanent traits (NEVER change across chapters)
**Gary:** tall presence; strong defined jawline; well-defined beard; kind warm eyes; confident grounded bearing; salt-and-pepper coloring.
**Jen:** warm genuine smile; bright expressive eyes; approachable elegance; long dark hair (or actual color).
**Together:** the candid chemistry from the couple photos — that ease is the through-line.

## The three character laws (from Bible §6)
1. **Recognizable gesture** once per chapter. 2. **Same age** throughout (~current age; Viking *weathered via light/wardrobe only*, not aged). 3. **Silver pendant** present every chapter.

## The Three Recognition Rules (from Bible §0c — every lifetime, subconscious)
1. **Gary notices Jen first** (soul-recognition, not attraction). 2. **Jen smiles second** — a beat of hesitation, *"I know you…"*. 3. **The pendant catches the light first** — light finds it, never the camera. Repeats across all ten lifetimes; the audience should feel it was always there.
